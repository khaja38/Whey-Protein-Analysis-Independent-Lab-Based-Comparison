#  Detailed Analysis

---

## 🧬 Data‑Driven Whey Protein Evaluation

**Source:** Public lab data (Trustified.in)
**Evaluator:** Independent fitness professional
**Goal:** Identify quality based on *ideal whey protein amino profile* + usability

---

### 🔎 Evaluation Standards

We evaluated 18 amino acids against ideal benchmarks:

* **Leucine (8–10%)** — muscle protein synthesis trigger
* **Lysine (7–9%)** — essential for recovery
* **Methionine & Tryptophan** — limiting EAAs, must be present
* **Glutamic Acid (12–15%)** — strong recovery & nitrogen balance
* Balanced presence of all EAAs and conditional essentials

We also considered:

* **BCAA total**
* **EAA total**
* **Actual usable protein**
* **Affordability**

---

## 📌 Product Assessments

### 🏆 Truebasics Clean Whey Isolate

**Performance:** ⭐ Best Balanced Protein
**Highlights:**

* Excellent balance of EAAs including leucine, lysine
* Strong BCAA vs usable protein
* Most aligned with ideal amino profile
  **Usable Protein:** 39.49
  **EAA:** 39.49 / BCAA: 18.04

### 🔥 The Whole Truth Whey Protein Isolate Unflavoured

**Highlights:**

  **EAA:** 40.77 / BCAA: 17.77

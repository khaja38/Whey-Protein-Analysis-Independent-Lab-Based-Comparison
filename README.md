# Whey-Protein-Analysis-Independent-Lab-Based-Comparison
Independent analysis of whey protein supplements using publicly available lab data (from Trustified.in). This project evaluates amino acid profiles, BCAA/EAA composition, and actual usable protein to identify high-quality products and inconsistencies in label claims.
Here’s a **clean, practical “ideal amino profile” cheat sheet** for whey protein — based on all 18 amino acids you listed 👇

---

# 🧬 What “best” looks like (ideal whey protein amino profile)

A top-quality whey protein should have:

### 🔑 Core Muscle Builders (EAA focus)

* **Leucine:** ~8–10% → main trigger for muscle growth
* **Isoleucine + Valine:** ~4–6% each → balanced BCAA support
* **Lysine:** ~7–9% → recovery, tissue repair
* **Threonine:** ~4–5% → protein balance, gut health
* **Histidine:** ~1–2% → basic completeness

---

### ⚠️ Limiting / Critical Aminos (must NOT be low)

* **Methionine:** ~1.5–2.5% → often underdosed
* **Tryptophan:** ~1–1.5% → mood, recovery, sleep
* **Phenylalanine + Tyrosine:** balanced (~2–4% each) → hormones + brain

---

### 🔋 Recovery & Performance Drivers

* **Glutamic Acid:** ~12–15% → nitrogen balance, recovery
* **Aspartic Acid:** ~7–10% → energy metabolism
* **Alanine:** ~3–5% → endurance support

---

### 🧱 Structural / Conditional Aminos

* **Arginine:** ~2–4% → blood flow, pump
* **Cysteine:** ~2–3% → antioxidant (glutathione)
* **Proline:** ~3–5% → connective tissue
* **Serine:** ~3–5% → recovery, metabolism
* **Glycine:** ~1–3% → repair, calming effect

---

# ⚖️ Balance Rules (Most Important)

* ✅ **No amino acid should be extremely low or missing**
* ✅ **BCAAs ≈ 20–25% of total protein**
* ✅ **EAAs ≈ 40–50% of total protein**
* ✅ **No artificial “spiking” (e.g., excess glycine/alanine)**
* ✅ **Smooth distribution > extreme highs/lows**

---

# 🚩 Red Flags in a Whey Protein

* ❌ Very low **methionine / tryptophan**
* ❌ Low **glutamic acid (<10%)**
* ❌ Extremely high **glycine or alanine** (possible amino spiking)
* ❌ Weak **leucine (<7%)**
* ❌ Big mismatch between label vs actual amino profile

---

# ✅ Simple Rule to Remember

👉 **“High leucine + strong EAA + balanced support aminos = truly complete whey protein”**
